My Portfolio

Detroit Venture Partners

Spent the summer after my freshman year in venture capital to learn the ropes. I need to know exactly what it means to sign a term sheet before I do so for my own endeavors.

UpTo

The world's first multi-layer calendar. Follow streams of your favorite content. Built out their Android Wear application and spent time growth hacking with the biz dev team.

Grand Circus

A community learning, enterprise training, and co-working space right next door to the M@dison. Worked to reinvigorate their entrepreneurial offerings alongside a BCG consultant.

Bitvendr

This hack developed at PennApps Spring 2014 allows users to use Venmo to buy bitcoin in less than 3 seconds. It was a huge success, but illegal in most states so we had to take it down.

Vybe

Vibrate your friends in real-time. Tap your phone's screen to send custom vibration patterns to anyone on your contacts list. Developed for Android 4.0.3 and above at Bitcamp 2014.

Samplify

Extract the DNA of your music with just one tap. Similar to Shazam, this application will identify songs it hears and provide you with start times and YouTube links to the samples used in the track.

SelfieAware

How much do you selfie? SelfieAware uses advanced facial recognition algorithms to identify your selfies on Instagram and calculates a "selfiness" score. Developed at LA Hacks 2014.

HackGT

Director of User Experience. Helped raise over $300k to finance Georgia Tech's premier hackathon. One of the top five hackathons this season with nearly 1000 participants. HackGT is going to be epic.

NocNoc

Wearables are expensive. NocNoc is an inexpensive alternative that turns your phone into a personal assistant with just a pair of headphones. Just knock on your pocket to get started.
